# 🧠 Soumya AI – Anonymous Healthcare Assistant 💚

Website Link: https://soumyaai.vercel.app/

## 🏆 Project Overview
**Soumya AI** is an anonymous, AI-powered healthcare guidance chatbot designed to address a critical gap in mental health support: people hesitate to seek help due to fear, stigma, and lack of privacy.  
Our solution provides **instant, anonymous, and judgment-free guidance**, acting as a *first point of support before professional intervention*.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
- Mental health issues are rising rapidly, especially among students and young adults.
- Many individuals avoid seeking help due to:
  - Social stigma  
  - Fear of being judged  
  - Data privacy concerns  
- Existing solutions often require login, identity, or stored personal data.

---

## 💡 Our Solution
Soumya AI offers:
- 🔐 **100% Anonymous Interaction** – No login, no identity, no stored chats  
- 🤖 **AI-Based Conversational Guidance** – Empathetic, structured responses  
- 🧠 **Mental Health First Aid** – Stress, anxiety, emotional clarity, self-help  
- 🌐 **Accessible Web Interface** – Simple, intuitive, and fast  

> ⚠️ Soumya AI does *not replace doctors* — it lowers the barrier to seeking help.

---

## 🚀 Key Innovation
- **Privacy-first design** from day one  
- **Blockchain-ready architecture** for trust & transparency  
- Planned **wallet-based access** instead of personal accounts  
- Designed for **scalability and real-world deployment**

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
**Frontend**
- Gradio (Python-based UI)  
- Custom HTML/CSS  

**Backend**
- Python  
- OpenAI & Gemini-compatible LLM API  

**Blockchain (Planned)**
- Monad Blockchain  
- MetaMask Wallet Integration  

**Deployment**
- Google Colab (development)  
- Web deployment (next phase)

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Ethics
- No user data stored  
- No personal identification required  
- No chat history saved  
- Clear medical disclaimer included  

Ethics and user safety are prioritized throughout development.

---

## 🌍 Impact & Use Cases
- Students hesitant to seek counseling  
- Individuals in remote or underserved areas  
- Early mental health intervention  
- Anonymous wellness check-ins
